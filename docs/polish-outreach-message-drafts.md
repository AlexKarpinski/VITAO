# Polish outreach message drafts

These drafts support the seven-day Polish market validation runbook for Issue #22. They are preparation only: do not record them as sent, published, or validated until a real outreach action occurs.

## Guardrails

- Use only a confirmed contact destination. Do not use `hello@vitao.studio` while it remains unverified.
- Replace every bracketed placeholder before sending.
- Do not promise pickup, exact dimensions, production time, durability, material properties, or availability unless separately confirmed.
- Keep the existing test-price wording (`od ... zł`) and describe it as an initial estimate, not a final quote.
- Record the channel and message variant in the validation log without committing personal recipient data.

## Marketplace reply — request for unavailable facts

Dziękuję za pytanie. Nie chcę podawać niepotwierdzonych parametrów. Dokładny wariant, rozmiar, termin i finalną cenę potwierdzę dopiero po ustaleniu potrzeb oraz możliwości wykonania.

## Marketplace reply — delivery

Dostawa na terenie Polski jest przewidziana. Dokładny koszt i sposób dostawy wymagają potwierdzenia dla konkretnego zamówienia. Zwykle odpowiadamy w ciągu 1–2 dni roboczych.

## Evidence to record after real use

For every sent or published variant, record:

- date and channel;
- variant name;
- product mentioned;
- response category: no response, general feedback, product interest, price objection, serious quote request;
- repeated questions or trust/contact friction;
- anonymized evidence link when available.

Do not prefill outcomes or convert these drafts into claimed customer feedback.
