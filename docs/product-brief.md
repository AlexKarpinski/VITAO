# VITAO Product Brief

## One-Line Concept

VITAO is a premium small studio brand for custom 3D-printed objects that make everyday spaces more organized, useful, and beautiful.

## What VITAO Is

VITAO sells curated and made-to-order small objects for modern homes, desks, studios, and routines.

The brand should communicate:

- thoughtful design
- practical everyday use
- premium presentation
- small-batch / made-to-order care
- customization without looking DIY or cheap

## What VITAO Is Not

VITAO is not:

- a generic 3D printing service
- a marketplace of random plastic objects
- a cheap tech hobby store
- a print farm website
- a full ecommerce platform at MVP stage

## Target Customer

Initial target customers:

- people who like clean home/desk organization
- remote workers and desk setup enthusiasts
- people looking for thoughtful small gifts
- gamers who want aesthetic stands and accessories
- beauty/cosmetic users who want neat organizers
- people who need custom-sized small functional objects

## MVP Conversion Goal

The first website version should convert visitors into conversations, not automated purchases.

Primary actions:

- request a custom piece
- ask about a product
- contact through email / Instagram / Telegram

Secondary actions:

- browse products
- understand materials and process
- learn who is behind the brand

## MVP Product Categories

Start with a small curated collection:

1. Desk & Office
2. Home Organization
3. Cosmetic & Vanity
4. Gaming & Display
5. Custom Pieces

## Example Products

- Ridge Tray
- Flow Pen Cup
- Modular Desk Organizer
- Dune Cosmetic Organizer
- Arc Controller Stand
- Elevate Headset Stand
- Wave Catch-All Dish
- Stackable Catch-All

## Pricing Approach

For MVP, use simple starting prices or price ranges:

- `From 29 zł`
- `From 49 zł`
- `Custom quote`

Avoid building checkout until demand is validated.

## Core Message

Custom printed objects for modern spaces.

Functional. Minimal. Made to fit your home, desk, studio, or routine.

## Site Tone

Use calm, premium, clear wording. Avoid overexplaining 3D printing. Focus on outcome and quality.

Preferred terms:

- custom printed objects
- made to order
- refined finish
- modern spaces
- functional design
- small studio
- thoughtful details

Avoid overusing:

- filament
- printer specs
- layer lines
- hobby printing
- cheap plastic
